Our Evolution Milestones

Our Glorious Healthcare Journey

Providing trusted healthcare across Nandyala region since inception.

Sanjeeva Hospital originally started as Sanjeeva Clinic - a humble, single-room OPD set-up dedicated entirely to out-patients. Driven by ethics and rural trust, we added 10 beds within 1 year, and scaled to a 20-bed hospital inside 2 years.

Over the past decade, we systematically brought specialized labs, operating rooms, high-tech pediatric wards (NICU/PICU), digital diagnostic platforms, and advanced surgical tools to serve Allagadda, Nandyala, Jammalamadugu, Banaganapalle, Mydukur, and neighboring villages.

Inception Year - Sanjeeva Clinic

Began as a small, one-room OPD section. Within 1 year, increased capacity to a 10-bed hospital setup. By the 2nd year, upgraded to a 20-bed unit to support inpatient recovery.

2016 - Laboratory & Diagnostics

Established a full clinical diagnostics laboratory to provide accurate medical testing internally and for nearby residents.

2018 - Operation Theatre Expansion

Launched sterilized operating rooms (OT services) for minor, general, and major surgical procedures under clinical conditions.

2020 - CPAP & HFNC Integration

Procured advanced pediatric life support systems including CPAP machines and High-Flow Nasal Cannula (HFNC) for the NICU division.

2022 - Advanced Ultrasound Launch

Integrated high-resolution clinical Ultrasound (USG) imaging to ensure reliable prenatal and internal organ diagnostics.

2024 - Modernized Surgical & Labs

Installed advanced Laparoscopic setups, state-of-the-art digital X-ray machines, and completely updated computerized laboratory services.

2026 - Premium 50-Bed Multispecialty Center

Grew into a fully-accredited, modern 50-bed healthcare unit offering specialized pediatric, gynecology, and critical trauma services.
