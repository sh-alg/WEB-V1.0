Our Medical Milestone

Serving Allagadda & Neighboring Villages Since 2016

In 2016, we began our healthcare journey with a small 20-bed clinic layout. Our strong pledge has always been to provide the most ethical diagnostic and treatment procedures for Allagadda, G V Textiles backside layouts, and all neighboring rural communities.

Thanks to the unwavering trust of our patients, we have expanded to become a premium 50-bed modern multispecialty facility. We offer pediatric incubators, Level II NICUs, general surgeries, and labor operation rooms under one safe roof.
